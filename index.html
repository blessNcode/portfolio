About Me

My name is Blessing Oyor. I was born in Lagos, Nigeria in 2008. I am a high school junior and an aspiring pilot and software engineer. I have been fascinated by technology for as long as I can remember. My passion grew from a young age with me taking my first coding class at 12 years old. This passion has led me to take computer science classes, intern in the Nextech Catapult Program, and become NCWIT's Indiana Affiliate Winner in 2025. Technology connects people on a global scale, breaking down borders and allowing us to share ideas, learn from different cultures, and collaborate with anyone, anywhere. It turns the world into a “global village,” where a student in Indiana can meet someone in Tokyo or Dubai and they can work together on a project, foster empathy, and spark innovation through shared creativity.
